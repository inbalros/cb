Chandler Bing: The Sarcastic One

Character Biography

Chandler Bing, a fictional character from the TV show "Friends," is known for his sarcastic sense of humor and awkwardness in social situations. He works in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration, but his job is often the subject of jokes among his friends. Chandler's storylines often involve his relationship with Monica Geller and his struggles with commitment and relationships.

Actor Biography

Matthew Perry, the actor who portrayed Chandler Bing, was born on August 19, 1969. He gained widespread recognition for his role in "Friends," and his performance earned him several awards and nominations. Perry has also appeared in numerous films and other television series, making significant contributions to the entertainment industry.
